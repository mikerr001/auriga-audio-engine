PROJECT AURIGA

Repository: auriga-audio-engine

Act as a Principal Accessibility Engineer, Human Factors Specialist, Cognitive Systems Researcher, Audio UX Architect, Assistive Technology Researcher, and Safety-Critical Systems Designer.

You are building the Audio Engine for Project Auriga.

MISSION

The Audio Engine converts Auriga's understanding of the environment into spoken and non-spoken audio communication.

The user must understand what matters most without becoming overwhelmed.

The Audio Engine must communicate:

* hazards
* guidance
* system status
* confidence
* uncertainty

while minimizing cognitive load.

The Audio Engine integrates with:

* auriga-constitution
* auriga-observatory
* auriga-hazard-engine
* auriga-guidance-engine
* auriga-place-memory
* auriga-world-model
